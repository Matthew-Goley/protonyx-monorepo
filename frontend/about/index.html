Vector

Vector is a desktop portfolio analytics application built for self-directed investors. It combines traditional financial analytics with the Lens engine - a modular analytical framework that processes your portfolio and delivers a ranked, prioritized set of actions.

All data is stored locally on your device. No brokerage link required. Nothing leaves your machine.

Lens Engine

Lens processes your portfolio and outputs a concise three-part brief: current state, context, and a single prioritized action. It runs Monte Carlo simulations to show how suggested changes affect portfolio risk, and generates a full projection list ranked by impact.

Protonyx is not a financial advisor. All Lens outputs are mathematical analysis and projections, not investment advice.

Overview

A new layer of intelligence for your portfolio.

Protonyx Vector is a desktop portfolio analytics application designed to combine traditional financial data with a more advanced analytical system called Vector Lens.

Built to help individuals better understand their portfolios through clean infrastructure, useful analytics, and focused outputs.

Purpose

Built for users who want answers, not dashboards.

  • Clarity over noise
  • Actionable insight over endless data
  • Simplicity without sacrificing intelligence

Vector Lens

An analytical engine built to turn data into direction.

Vector Lens processes thousands of data points across your portfolio and surfaces what matters — not raw figures, but structured insight you can act on.

01

Lens Brief

Three sentences. The single most important thing happening in your portfolio, right now.

  • Current state — trend, slope, volatility, and direction
  • Context — dividends, EPS, and event inputs
  • Projection — a concrete next step, grounded in math
02

Lens Projections

A ranked set of portfolio adjustments, each derived from the full Lens analysis. Specific. Quantified. Actionable.

  • Generated from real portfolio conditions
  • Tied directly to your positions and gaps
  • Updated as your portfolio evolves
03

Mathematical Analysis

Two Monte Carlo simulations — your portfolio as-is, and with projections applied — rendered side by side to show the math behind every suggestion.

  • Visualize distribution tightening
  • See projected risk reduction clearly
  • Every output backed by simulation
